# ISSUE-003: Relationship Visibility May Be Too Local

## Workflow

Review of README, Study model, Codex commands, and relationship surfaces.

## Reproduction

1. Read the relationship-related README sections.
2. Inspect Study investigation fields and Codex relationship commands.
3. Ask whether a writer in Write mode can perceive intellectual structure without entering a separate workflow.

## Screenshots

Pending long-session screenshots.

## Philosophical Impact

Essai wants to strengthen intellectual structure. If relationships are hidden in mode-specific surfaces, the structure exists computationally but may not become part of the writer's lived awareness.

## Usability Impact

Users may duplicate notes, miss relevant source evidence, or fail to notice orphaned concepts.

## Technical Observations

The data model has relationship vocabulary, but the review has not yet confirmed persistent cross-mode visibility.

## Suggested Direction

Design a restrained relationship inspector that distinguishes direct, inferred, proposed, and broken links without becoming a dashboard.
