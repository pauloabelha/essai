# Workflow Review

## Write To Study

The ideal transition: a writer notices a concept or uncertainty, moves into Study, finds source-grounded passages, and returns with clearer evidence without losing the manuscript's thread.

Risk:
- If Study search feels like a separate research app, the author may lose the sentence they were carrying.

Review task:
- Draft a paragraph, move to Study for one phrase, inspect a PDF passage, create a note, and return to the paragraph.

## Study To Codex

The Study passage handoff into Codex is one of Essai's best workflow ideas. It turns search evidence into a durable workspace entry with provenance.

Risk:
- Handoffs can create loose fragments. The review should inspect whether staged excerpts become findable again later.

## Source To Note

Source capture and note capture are both visible in the right pane. This supports fieldwork-like rhythm: read, capture, continue.

Risk:
- If capture status messages or source indexing feedback interrupt the writing center, the rhythm may become administrative.

## Codex Workspace

The Codex workspace is a shared editable Markdown surface, not only a chat. This is deeply aligned with Essai: durable writing beats ephemeral conversation.

Risk:
- Conversation history is durable JSON, but the most readable durable artifact is Markdown. Long reviews should test whether important Codex insights naturally migrate into workspace notes.
